Musical Director, Mel Wilkinson

The Meltones’ origins were at Sir John Deane’s College Adult Education Department back in 2008. It was Mel’s first choir that she led, so it holds a special place in her heart. There are still some of the original members singing all these years later. It is a choir that is first and foremost about the joy of singing. However, we strive for a good standard and sing in three-part harmony, Musical Theatre, Pop and a little bit of Classical too!
